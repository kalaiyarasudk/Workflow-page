Four-Level Approval Flow

Access & Responsibility Approval Portal

Streamlined, auditable access requests with AppSec validation, App Owner oversight, and Sysadmin fulfillment. Each step captures comments, attachments, and status changes for compliance-ready traceability.

Workflow Snapshot

User Request

Submit SSO-authenticated request with job details and supporting templates for creation, responsibility changes, or removal.

AppSec Validation (L1)

Review data, comment on issues, or approve when compliant. Push back incorrect submissions to user with actionable feedback.

App Owner Review (A/B)

Owners ensure business relevance and approve responsibilities. Any rejection loops back to AppSec for further action.

Sysadmin Fulfillment

Auto-generate ServiceNow RITM, apply changes, and attach evidence, closing the loop with final notifications.

Role Workspaces

AppSec (L1 Approver)

  • Validation checklist & SLA timer
  • Reject with comments or approve
  • Push back to user for corrections

App Owners A/B

  • View AppSec notes and attachments
  • Parallel approvals with audit log
  • Decision required from both A and B

Sysadmin

  • Auto ServiceNow RITM creation
  • Execution notes & evidence upload
  • Completion notifications to stakeholders
